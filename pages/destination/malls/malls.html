Market! Market!

It is a bustling shopping destination located in the heart of Bonifacio Global City (BGC), Taguig. Known for its unique mix of local and international brands, it offers an exciting and diverse shopping experience for everyone.

Venice Grand Canal Mall

It is a premier lifestyle destination located in McKinley Hill, Taguig City. Inspired by the romantic charm of Venice, Italy, the mall is famous for its stunning replica of the Venetian Grand Canal, complete with gondola rides and beautifully designed bridges.

SM Aura Premier

It is an upscale shopping mall located in the bustling district of Bonifacio Global City (BGC), Taguig. Known for its sleek and modern architecture, the mall offers a sophisticated shopping and lifestyle experience with a blend of high-end retail brands, trendy fashion outlets, and fine dining options.

Uptown Mall

It is a modern and luxurious shopping destination located in Uptown Bonifacio, Taguig City. Known for its elegant design and upscale atmosphere, the mall offers a diverse range of high-end fashion brands, gourmet dining, and entertainment options, catering to a discerning crowd of shoppers and visitors.
